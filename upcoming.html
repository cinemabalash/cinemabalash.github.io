DOUBLE FEATURE

Following the Opening of Opening, Cinema Balash presents double bills of the full features from which opening scenes were drawn from during the opening night.

The first in this series will be Ingmar Bergman's "The Magic Flute" and Juzo Itami's "Tampopo".

The Magic Flute

Considered the greatest adaptation of opera to film Bergman's 1975 movie brings Mozart's work to the cinema not as theater, a stage, built and performed on for a camera, theatrical effects displayed through cinematic means.


Tampopo

Itami's "Tampopo" 'Ramen Western' is a sincere celebration of food, sex and film genres.
It is one of the most life- and film- affirming movies ever made. Don't miss this chance to see it on the big screen!
